STEM for Change Scholarship Program

Up to C$100,000 in scholarships available to women applying to study STEM at an UNIWOLC Canadian partner school.

About the Scholarship

UNIWOLC Is Very Excited To Announce Its STEM For Change Scholarship Program! Our Goal Is To Drive Diversity And Inclusion By Empowering International Women To Pursue An Education In Science, Technology, Engineering, And Mathematics. Through This Merit-Based Scholarship Program, UNIWOLC Will Award Over C$100,000 In Scholarships To Selected Applicants.
